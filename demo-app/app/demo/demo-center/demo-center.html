Center

Use

The center component is used for aligning other components into the center of the parent element.

Sample

This is centered text.
<ign-center>This is centered text.</ign-center>

Content

  • Elements that are centered include buttons and text in dialog components, images, and other components at the discretion of the developer.

Style

  • The center component is used for centering elements without needing to utilize the center property in css. The css center property is not needed and should be avoided when utilizing this component.
