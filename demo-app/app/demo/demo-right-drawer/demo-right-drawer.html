Right Drawer


Use

Right drawer components are used to display summarized information and navigational linkage to other pages and components when a selected item is clicked on.

           [startOpen]="true">

             key="1234"

             subtitle="I am a right drawer subtitle."
        >
        </ign-list-selection-item>
