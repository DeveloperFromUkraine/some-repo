Routes

The following are page level, route name guidelines should be adhered to when implementing new pages.

Accessibility Requirements

  • The route name is set as the title of the webpage in the tab and is spoken when the page is opened
    • Consider how the route will sound when spoken. Acronyms such as 'TOA' would not be understood by accessibility readers.
    • Try to use words that are not shortened or abbreviated for the best accessibility experience
    • Keep your route names short yet descriptive
