AAA Pattern

Arrange variables, mocks and data needed for the test to run. Add common variables, mocks and data to beforeEach() block.

Act on the class or method under test.

Assert to make sure that your expectations are met.

            
it('should call closeDrawer when click event emitted', () => {{ '{' }}
    //Arrange
    de = fixture.debugElement.query(By.css('.close-icon'));
    ne = de.nativeElement;
    jest.spyOn(component, 'closeDrawer');
    
    //Act
    ne.dispatchEvent(new Event('click'));

    //Assert
    expect(component.closeDrawer).toHaveBeenCalled();
});
